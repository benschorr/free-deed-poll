Change Your Name for Free

In the UK, you can legally change your name by signing your own deed poll. This page helps you create a basic deed poll document that you can print, sign and have witnessed.

Important: This is a general DIY template and does not constitute legal advice. It is designed for straightforward adult name changes. If your situation is complex (children, court orders, immigration issues, safety concerns, etc.), you should use a professional service.

What to Do After Printing

  • Sign the deed poll using your new name and your old name.
  • Have both witnesses sign and add their full names and addresses.
  • Keep the original safe and make copies for organisations.
